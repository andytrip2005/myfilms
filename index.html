Film Projects

A collection of short films I’ve created and directed

Intro to Frat Life

This is a short documentary film I directed. With some raw footage and sit-down interviews, it explores the culture and brotherhood of a university fraternity.

Uptown Skating Rink

A silent short film capturing a winter day at the Uptown Waterloo ice rink. Through visual storytelling and candid shots, I aimed to evoke a sense of warmth in the cold cold winter.

Moon King

A short indie film created by the UW Film Club. I directed and starred in this experimental piece that explores identity, surrealism, and symbolism under moonlight. If you ask for my personal opinion; It's a trip.

Analogue Horror

A short film I thought of, directed and starred in; from 12 am - 3 am after I realized my project of creating a short film was overdue and I would be presenting it the next day. This piece explores an interesting activity taking place in my basement.
